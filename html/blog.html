Technology

And it's effects on my daily life


Technology as defined by Oxford English Dictionary is the branch of knowledge that deals with the creation and use of technical means and their interrelation with life, society, and the environment, drawing upon such subjects as industrial arts, engineering, applied science, and pure science.

Nevertheless, the equivalent connotation of technology alters depending on the context. Technology can be represented as the benefit of scientific knowledge to recast and exploit the human atmosphere. Technology can also be characterized as a field of proficiency accountable for the expansion and implementation of technological processes and their relationship with society and the environment, using engineering, applied science, and celibacy. The expression technology is emanated from the antique Greek terms "techne" and "logos". The term technology was used to associate scientific progress and achievement in various fields.


Technology is an absolute need that we cannot escape, and it plays a very important role in many aspects of our lives. Over the centuries technology has changed. The term technology does not have a neutral intent. Depending on their perspective and background different people will interpret it differently. Technology is the way through which we applied knowledge and transferred the techniques tools and machines for practical purposes. Technology's importance and influence in our personal and professional lives are all the time understanding how people use technology and how technology and people relationship with one another. Technology is not just important for those who study create and introduce new technology but also for all those who use those technologies.


As the globe locomotes swiftly, more progressive features are furnished. Technology has influenced me in a positive manner. I am e able to communicate anytime, anywhere. Furthermore, the smartphone I use, has more features, more games, more fun, and is now given the same technology as having a computer and is cheap and better and faster and more reliable. Technology has transformed my daily life by giving me access to amazing tools and resources. I am able to surf the internet, play games, talk to my friends, watch movies, and much more. All these technological advances have made our lives better, easier, faster, and more satisfying.All during pandemic of Covid-19, I was able to continue my studies through laptop and online application called Zoom. Technology made this possible to have a long distance interaction with my teacher, module leaders and friends. Technology has revolutionized the way we can have fun, share, and use all kinds of information. Technology has also helped me to travel to many new locations I am unaware of.
